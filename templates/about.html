About VYNOX

Transforming Placement Preparation Through Intelligent Assessment

VYNOX is an AI-powered Placement Readiness System that bridges the gap between academic learning and industry requirements. Our platform evaluates technical skills and provides personalized roadmaps for career success.

Our Mission

To democratize placement preparation by providing every student with personalized, data-driven insights into their technical skills and career readiness.

  • Accurate skill assessment through AI
  • Personalized learning paths
  • Industry-relevant project guidance
  • Continuous progress tracking

Our Vision

To become the world's leading platform for placement readiness, empowering millions of students to achieve their dream careers through intelligent assessment and guidance.
